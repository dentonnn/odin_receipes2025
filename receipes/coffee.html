Hong Kong Style Coffee Recipe

Step by step guide on how to make HK style coffee

  1. Brew the Coffee: Make a strong cup of black coffee.
  2. Add Milk and Sweetener: To the brewed tea, add a portion of evaporated milk and sweetened condensed milk.
    3. Brew the Tea: Steep black tea in hot water until it's strong.
  3. Stir well and Mix
  4. Enjoy hot or cold
